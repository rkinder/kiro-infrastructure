# TELOS

Main TELOS framework document - overview of your life operating system.

## What is TELOS?

TELOS (Telic Evolution and Life Operating System) is your personal context system. It captures who you are, what you believe, what you're working toward, and what you've learned.

## Purpose

This system helps AI assistants understand your context without re-explaining every time. It's your persistent memory across sessions.

## Structure

- **BELIEFS.md** - Core beliefs and principles
- **BOOKS.md** - Reading list
- **CHALLENGES.md** - Current obstacles
- **FRAMES.md** - Conceptual perspectives
- **GOALS.md** - Life goals and objectives
- **LESSONS.md** - Lessons learned
- **MISSION.md** - Personal mission statement
- **MODELS.md** - Mental models and frameworks
- **MOVIES.md** - Film recommendations
- **NARRATIVES.md** - Key stories and themes
- **PREDICTIONS.md** - Future predictions
- **PROBLEMS.md** - Problems you're solving
- **PROJECTS.md** - Active and planned projects
- **STRATEGIES.md** - Approaches to goals
- **TELOS.md** - Main framework overview
- **TRAUMAS.md** - Past traumas (optional)
- **WISDOM.md** - Accumulated wisdom
- **WRONG.md** - Things you were wrong about

## How to Use

1. Fill out the files that resonate with you (not all are required)
2. Update them as you learn and grow
3. Reference them when working with AI assistants
4. Let them evolve over time
